Who are we?

ClipperCardDevs is an informal group of developers gathering around resources and technologies to improve usage of the MTC Clipper Card. We are unofficial (NOT endorsed by MTC nor clippercard.com) but enthusiastic about making the lives of transit users easier.

Visit our Github Org!

Why?

Improve Access to Clipper Data

We aim to collect current technology to make development around the Clipper card easier.

Improve Future Policy

We aim to communicate with MTC and lobby for pro-tech changes to policy and usage.

Developer Support

We aim to assist developers looking to integrate Clipper data into their applications.

Open and Transparent

We aim to release all our work open source. Visit our Github Org to take a look.
